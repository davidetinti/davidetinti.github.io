Davide Tinti

I am a Master's degree in Computer Science student at University of Bologna, specializing in software engineering.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Bologna and over 4 years of experience in FullStack and Mobile development, as well as in the Internet of Things sector, particularly in the Web of Things. My skills include software architecture and database design.

I'm passionate about project management in the IT field, from feasibility studies to innovative problem-solving through emerging technologies and communication protocols.
